Explore 3 Distinct Areas

Travel through three connected areas each spanning multiple levels with a distinct look and feel. Discover story points built into the level design as you fend off monsters and beasts unique to each area. 

Wield Different Weapons

Choose your weapon of favour and become more powerful as you progress. Each weapon has its own advantages and provides challenging gameplay for each situation.

Slay Evil Beasts

Encounter dangerous creatures throughout your journey which will make keeping your life difficult. Each area has unique creatures to surprise you along the way. Many of them hungry. All of them evil.

Battle Trecherous Bosses

No area is complete without a large beast guarding the next.
